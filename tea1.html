Green Maté

Between green tea and coffee.

Description

An excellent green maté, the Original Maté is our flagship product, a green maté that appeals to both those discovering maté and the greatest enthusiasts of this beverage.

Each sip reveals a taste halfway between green tea and coffee, perfectly balanced, immersing you in the heart of South American rituals.

No frills, just the pure essence of maté. Natural, balanced, and deeply rooted in tradition. It’s the ideal companion for those seeking a natural and powerful drink, perfect for starting the day with energy and mental clarity.

Our matés are certified 100% natural and free from artificial flavors.
