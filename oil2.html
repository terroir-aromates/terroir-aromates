Pink Berries

Energizing.

Description

Pink berries: Originating from Central and South America, pink berry oil is highly prized in perfumery and by athletes before intense exertion. It is spicy, with cumin notes, and can be used in cooking or for topical application.

Pink berries, also known as pink pepper, originate from South America, particularly Brazil and Peru, where they grow wild.

They come from the Schinus terebinthifolius tree, which can reach a height of 3 to 10 meters.

Pink berries were introduced to Europe by explorers and merchants, intrigued by their pink appearance and unique flavor.
