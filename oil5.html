Lime Oil

Anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic.

Description

The essential oil of lime from South America is derived from the peel of the fruit of Citrus aurantifolia, a shrub native to Southeast Asia and now cultivated in South America.

It is obtained by distilling the peel of the fruits, which are hand-harvested and washed before being peeled to extract the zest.

This essential oil is known for its antiseptic, antiviral, immune-stimulating, tonic, and nerve-soothing properties.

It is used in aromatherapy, perfumery, skin and hair care, treatment of respiratory infections, and relief from stress and anxiety.

Usage

Anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic
