Cynara Oil

Antioxidant and detoxifying.

Description

Cynara oil: Used to support liver health, this oil comes from South America and is one of the many essential oils produced in this region.

As a dietary supplement, cynara oil is used for its liver-supporting and detoxifying effects. It is recommended during seasonal changes or after periods of dietary excess.

It contains silymarin, which protects the liver and promotes bile flow.

Cynara oil is also used in body care products, such as body oils that combine cynara oil with other precious oils like argan oil for firm, radiant skin.

These products are often enriched with essential oils for their refreshing and soothing effects.

In summary, cynara oil is a versatile ingredient with applications in cosmetics, nutrition, and dietary supplements, offering benefits for the skin, liver, and overall health.
