Veracruzano Premium Export

High-altitude coffee with an unmatched flavor and aroma.

Description

Welcome!

We are suppliers of high-altitude Coatepec coffee, ideal for cafes, restaurants, hotels, or simply for those who love good coffee anywhere.

Our beans are carefully selected from the best coffee-growing regions of Veracruz, ensuring an unmatched flavor and aroma.

Our coffees are roasted daily to preserve maximum freshness. These are not batch-roasted coffees stored for months or years. We aim to replicate the experience of coming to Coatepec to select your coffee in an authentic traditional café.

Our coffee comes from farms located over 1300 meters above sea level, in the cloud forest of Veracruz. This is not industrially grown coffee, meaning our cups are sweet and have an exquisite body.
