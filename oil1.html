Palo Santo

Anti-inflammatory.

Description

Palo Santo is a traditional natural remedy that you can apply to your skin or burn as incense to reduce pain, inflammation, and stress.

Originating from Central and South America, palo santo, or "holy wood," is used by shamans and medicine men in purification rituals. It has analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties due to its high myrcene content. Palo Santo (Bursera graveolens) is a tree native to Peru, Ecuador, and other South American countries. It grows in dry tropical forests and produces a highly fragrant resin.

Palo Santo essential oil comes from the harvest of the fruit of this South American region. The collection is done by hand and only from mature trees. The fruits are then steam-distilled to obtain a mystical essential oil with citrus and menthol accents.
