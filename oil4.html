Lemon Balm Oil

Sedative and relieves nervous tension.

Description

This oil is considered an ultra-powerful sedative and is produced in South America. Lemon balm essential oil is obtained by steam distillation of the leaves of the Melissa officinalis plant, known for its calming and sedative properties.

It is used for various applications, including aiding digestion, reducing hypertension, and relieving nervous tension.

Lemon balm essential oil can be used in different ways, such as topical application after dilution with a carrier oil, inhalation, or diffusion.

It is also used in culinary preparations, but it is important to respect dosages and preparation methods.
