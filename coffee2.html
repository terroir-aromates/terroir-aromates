Colombian Supremo Coffee

An exceptional coffee from the high mountains of the Colombian Andes.

Description

Coffee grown in the high mountains of the Colombian Andes, where temperature, solar radiation, and the quality of the land make it one of the most privileged coffees in the world.

Enjoy a cup of coffee with a sweet and fragrant aroma, accompanied by accentuated acidity, fruity and floral notes, and excellent smoothness with a velvety body.

Perfect for those who enjoy a cup of coffee with a strong body and vibrant, lemony acidity. Ideal to savor at your favorite time of the day.

Colombian Supremo Coffee represents tradition and innovation in the coffee world. 'Colombiano' comes from coffees grown in Colombia, carefully transported to be roasted on-site, ensuring every step of the process. It contains 100% strict-height Arabica beans, with no added flavors, offering a comforting and unique beverage.

Our master roasters provide a medium roast level to highlight the best qualities of Colombian coffee.

Why Choose Colombian Supremo Coffee?

  • Grown in the Colombian Andes at high altitude
  • 100% premium Arabica beans
  • Sweet aroma with fruity and floral notes
  • Medium roast for perfect balance
  • Ideal for a strong body and lemony acidity

Benefits

  • Authentic Colombian origin
  • Traditional on-site roasting process
  • Velvety body and unique flavor
  • Perfect for espresso or daily enjoyment
  • No additives, 100% natural
